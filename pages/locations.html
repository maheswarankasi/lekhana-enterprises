Locations

🌍 Lekhana Enterprises: Extensive Service Area Coverage

Lekhana Enterprises is proud to offer professional gas pipeline installation services across a wide and strategically important geographical area, primarily focused on Tamil Nadu and extending into neighboring regions. Our strong, localized presence ensures that we can provide prompt, reliable, and expert service regardless of your project's location within our service zones.

📍 Primary Service Regions

We cover major cities and industrial hubs, ensuring our specialized services are accessible where they are needed most.

Tamil Nadu

  • Coimbatore Metropolitan Area: We offer comprehensive coverage for all four quadrants: North, South, East, and West Coimbatore. This extensive focus allows us to cater effectively to both the commercial core and the expanding residential suburbs.
  • Salem: A key area for our commercial and industrial gas pipeline work.
  • Karur: Serving this textile and manufacturing hub with reliable gas infrastructure.
  • Hosur: Strategically important for industrial and warehousing installations near the Karnataka border.
  • Dindigul & Madurai Corridor: Connecting the central city of Dindigul to the major metropolitan area of Madurai for project development and service calls.

🏘️ Town and District Coverage

Our commitment extends beyond the major cities to crucial surrounding districts and regional towns, ensuring no client is left underserved.

  • Erode: Covering the industrial and commercial demands of the entire Erode district.
  • Tiruppur: Essential service for the "Textile Valley" of India, catering to industrial and process gas needs.
  • The Palani Region: Including the key towns of Palani, Pollachi and Udumalaipet, serving both domestic and commercial clients in this agricultural and industrial belt.
  • Nilgiris & Foothills: Service coverage extending to Ooty, Mettupalayam, and Karamadai, addressing the unique installation requirements of varied elevations and terrain.

Neighboring States

  • Palakkad, Kerala: Leveraging our proximity to the Tamil Nadu border, we extend our reliable gas pipeline installation services into the Palakkad district of Kerala, catering to cross-border project requirements.
